What is iTabs?

iTabs is an open-source information display system that makes it easy to share and consume complex information in a user-friendly, interactive format.

Our flagship feature is the üëçi button - a simple, familiar interface that transforms static information into dynamic, interactive content.

Our Mission

We believe information should be accessible, engaging, and contextual. Our mission is to transform how people interact with online content by:

  • Reducing information overload
  • Presenting content in bite-sized, focused chunks
  • Keeping users in their flow without disruption
  • Making complex information easily digestible

How It Works

iTabs uses a combination of familiar interfaces (like emojis and information icons) with modern web technologies to create seamless information experiences.

Our system is designed to be lightweight, accessible, and easy to implement on any website or platform.

Key Benefits

iTabs provides significant advantages for both content creators and consumers:

  • Keep users on your site longer with contextual information
  • Increase engagement through interactive elements
  • Improve information retention with focused content
  • Enhance user experience with seamless interactions
